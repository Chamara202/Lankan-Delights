About Us

At Lankan Delights we believe in creating an unforgettable dining experience for every guest. Mahiyanganaya city our restaurant combines specific theme and atmosphere to provide a warm and inviting ambiance. Our journey began 1995 driven by a passion for culinary excellence and a commitment to serving delectable dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a special dinner celebration, or simply craving our renowned chicken biryani we promise a feast for your senses. Led by our talented Executive Chef Jagath Kamalanath our kitchen team crafts each dish with creativity and skill, ensuring every bite is a culinary delight. From our carefully curated menu to our attentive service, every aspect of your dining experience is designed to exceed your expectations. Beyond our food, we take pride in fostering a welcoming environment where friends and families can come together to enjoy great food and create lasting memories. We invite you to relax, indulge, and savor the flavors of Lankan Delights. Thank you for choosing Lankan Delights. We look forward to serving you.
